typography.line-height

Line Height

Line height controls readability and vertical rhythm. Too tight makes text feel claustrophobic; too loose loses paragraph cohesion.

Correct — 1.5× for body

Raise capital quickly and efficiently by finding the right lending partners for any debt requirement on Yubi Marketplace. Multiple expressions of interest, guaranteed.

line-height: 1.5 (24px at 16px)
Caution — 1.2× for body

Raise capital quickly and efficiently by finding the right lending partners for any debt requirement on Yubi Marketplace. Multiple expressions of interest, guaranteed.

line-height: 1.2 — only for headings
Wrong — 2.0× for body

Raise capital quickly and efficiently by finding the right lending partners for any debt requirement. Guaranteed.

line-height: 2.0 — too loose, use 1.5

typography.measure

Line Length

Optimal line length (measure) is 60–80 characters for body text. Longer lines increase eye tracking strain; shorter lines fragment reading flow.

Too short (<45ch) Optimal (60–80ch) Too long (>90ch)
